Planning your holiday

Here are some frequently asked questions which will help you plan your stay

When are we open?
we are open from 2nd Jan to 22nd December every year.
Where are we located?
we are located in Sandymount, Dublin 2. Just steps from a lovely sandy beach.
How can I get there?
We are about 25 mins by taxi from the airport. We are 15 mins from both Heuston and Connelly train stations. The 34 and 34A buses from the city centre stop about 20 yrds from our house. It would take about 30 mins to walk from the city centre.
When is check-in?
Regular check-in is 12-6pm each day. If you plan to arrive outside these times, please let us know so we can arrange your check-in.
When is breakfast served
Breakfast is served from 7:30-10am on weekdays, or 7-10am at the weekend.
